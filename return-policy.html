Return Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Returns Window

You may return eligible items within 15 days of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached.

2. Eligible Items

Most apparel, footwear, and accessories are eligible for return. The following items are not returnable:

  • Innerwear, swimwear, and lingerie (for hygiene reasons)
  • Earrings and pierced jewelry
  • Custom-made or altered products
  • Gift cards
  • Items marked "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" on the product page
3. How to Initiate a Return

Log in to your ShopMax account, go to My Orders, select the item you wish to return, and click Return. You will receive a return confirmation and pickup details via email within 24 hours.

4. Pickup & Shipping

We offer free doorstep pickup for all returns within our serviceable areas. Please ensure the item is securely packed in its original packaging. Our courier partner will collect it within 2–4 business days from the date of request.

5. Refund Timeline

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days. The amount will be credited to your original payment method or ShopMax wallet, whichever you choose. See our Refund Policy for details.

6. Exchange

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you wish to exchange an item, please return it for a refund and place a new order for the desired item.

7. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund at your preference.

8. Contact Us

For return-related queries, reach us at support@shopmax.in or call 1800-123-SHOP (10 AM – 8 PM, Mon–Sat).
