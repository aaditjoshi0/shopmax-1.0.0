Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. When Refunds Are Issued

Refunds are issued for the following scenarios:

  • Items returned within the 15-day return window and found to be in original condition
  • Damaged, defective, or incorrect items received
  • Orders cancelled before shipment
  • Orders that could not be delivered due to an address issue (after 3 delivery attempts)
2. Refund Amount

The refund amount equals the purchase price paid for the item(s), including any applicable taxes. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (damaged/defective/incorrect item). Return shipping costs are covered by ShopMax for eligible returns.

4. Wallet Refunds

If you opt for a ShopMax Wallet refund, the amount is credited instantly upon return approval and can be used for your next purchase. Wallet credits do not expire.

5. Non-Refundable Items

Items marked "Final Sale", innerwear, swimwear, pierced jewelry, gift cards, and custom-made products are non-refundable. Please review the product page carefully before purchase.

6. Partial Refunds

A partial refund may be issued if an item is returned in a condition that does not meet our guidelines (e.g., missing tags, visible wear, odor). You will be notified via email before a partial refund is processed.

7. Contact Us

For refund-related queries, email refunds@shopmax.in or call 1800-123-SHOP. Please have your order ID ready.
