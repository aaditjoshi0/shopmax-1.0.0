Delivery Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Delivery Areas

We deliver to all pin codes across India. Orders to metropolitan cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Pune, Ahmedabad) typically arrive faster. Remote and hilly areas may require additional transit time.

2. Delivery Charges

Delivery charges are calculated automatically at checkout based on the order value:

  • Orders under ₹500 — ₹50 delivery fee
  • Orders between ₹500 and ₹999 — ₹30 delivery fee
  • Orders of ₹1,000 or more — FREE delivery
3. Estimated Delivery Time
LocationStandard DeliveryExpress Delivery*
Metro Cities3–5 business days1–2 business days
Other Cities5–7 business days2–3 business days
Remote / Hilly Areas7–10 business daysNot available

* Express delivery is available at an additional ₹99 charge and can be selected during checkout for eligible pin codes.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email and SMS with a tracking number. You can also track your order in real time from the My Orders section of your ShopMax account.

5. Shipping Partners

We partner with leading courier services such as Delhivery, Blue Dart, and India Post to ensure reliable and timely delivery across India.

6. Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery is unsuccessful after 3 attempts, the package will be returned to us. You will be refunded the order value (excluding shipping charges) after we receive the returned package. Please ensure your address and phone number are correct at the time of ordering.

7. Order Modification

Once an order is placed, the shipping address cannot be modified. If you need to change your delivery address, please cancel the order (before shipment) and place a new one with the correct details.

8. Contact Us

For delivery-related queries, email delivery@shopmax.in or call 1800-123-SHOP (10 AM – 8 PM, Mon–Sat).
